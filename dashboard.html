Activation Gap: Only 40.4% of registered advisers have ever made a referral. The largest never-referred cohort is on the SJP platform (6,342 advisers). This is the single largest growth lever: improving activation from 40% to 50% would add over 1,300 new active advisers.
Firm Dead Weight: 49.6% of registered firms have made zero referrals. This likely includes firms that registered but never onboarded properly. A firm-level activation audit — cross-referencing registration date and any CRM connection — could identify the highest-priority targets.
